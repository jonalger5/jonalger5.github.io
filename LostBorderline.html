Borderline Mechanic

  • Type: Mechanic

  • Allows the player to wrap around select edges of the screen, in order to strategically maneuver each dungeon

Crystal Golem

  • Type: Enemy

  • Upon death, if the Golem is not at the bottom tier, then the Golem can split into two lower tiers

  • After an amount of time has passed, two lower tiers of the Golem can merge together to form a higher tier of the Golem

  • Utilizes pathfinding to chase the player around obstacles and through the Borderlines

Steam Turret

  • Type: Obstacle

  • A stationary obstacle that sprays steam in a Clockwise/Counter-Clockwise rotation

  • Steam damages player upon contact but the player can stop the steam by shooting/slashing the Steam Turret's base

Mine Cart

  • Type: Interactable/Obstacle

  • Carries a Wall Switch that can be shot by the player to lower designated walls in the dungeon

  • Incorporates a Serializable route that can be dynamically changed by developers

  • Upon contact, damages and teleports the player to a set location
