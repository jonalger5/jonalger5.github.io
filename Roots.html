Roots - Summer 2017

"Discover your roots and heal a wounded forest in this game where Super Mario Bros. meets Ori and the Blind Forest!"

Made with Unity(C#)

Lead Engineer - Team Size: 4


Contributions:

Sapling

  • Type: Main Character

  • Developed the movement and interaction system

Sporeling

  • Type: Enemy

  • Damages player upon contact but can be killed if the player jumps on top of the Sporeling

  • Incorporated patroling behavior to detect platform edges and walls

Sporog

  • Type: Enemy

  • Can't be killed but allows the player to jump higher if the player jumps on top of the Sporog

  • Incorporated patroling behavior to detect platform edges and walls

Other Contributions

  • Developed the Game Manager to handle all game loop logic

  • Implemented Tutorial help to inform the player about the game

  • Created the behavior for the different tiles in the game which can damage the player, increase the player's jump or disappear upon player contact

  • Incorporated collectable key components which allowed the player to advance to the next level

  • Added Checkpoints to allow the player to save their progress in the level
